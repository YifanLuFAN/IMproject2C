This week we learned about text deformation (turning text into dots), and I also wanted to try to create something innovative with text.
I didn't make any complex changes to the text style, but I had a new idea about the movement of the text. I made the text move in a "roller-like" way, and the text was of course my name. This 3D change makes the picture look very three-dimensional. For the color, the system randomly generates it in a selected range.
The speed and rotation radius of the text movement can be freely adjusted, and the text content can also be freely selected, which is very interesting.

Idea for Final

Chose Your Story

My final project was inspired by the game Detroit: Become Human. I wanted to make a "playable video" (built with HTML). After watching a short film, the player can enter different story lines by choosing different options and finally reach different endings.
My story is "A mouse ran into your room!". The player will play the role of a student who is afraid of mice. In the middle of the night, strange noises wake you up from your sleep. You can search different places in the room to find different weapons to defeat the mouse.

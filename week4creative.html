I just started to use Processing this week and I am not familiar with it yet, so I did not make a very complex website.
Combining what I learned in class, I made a website that can draw random shapes. When users press and hold the mouse and drag, they can create shapes on the screen along the mouse track. Each time the mouse is clicked, the generated shape will be randomly selected from three shapes: triangle, rectangle, and ellipse, and the color will be randomly determined. The size of the shape will change with MouseX and MouseY.
In class, we learned to let the system generate random shapes by itself, and my idea is to make this random process controllable through mouse clicks and movements.

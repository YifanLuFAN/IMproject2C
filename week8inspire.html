Inspiration

CHESS ROBOT

A robotic arm that plays chess! This project integrates aspects of robotics, computer vision, and of course, chess. Student were tasked with creting their own own robotic arm using a variety of fabrication techniques such as laser cutting and 3D printing to produce unique designs.
The final chessboard was scaled down in size. This was due to limitations in 3D-priting space and concerns regarding stability. The robotic arm is a Selective Compliance Articulated Robot Arm (SCARA), which has three degress of freedom.
